Question of

True or False?

There are two types of treaties: historic and modern. Modern treaties are also called comprehensive land claim agreements (CLCA).

Answer: True

Starting in 1975 a new era of treaty making began. As mentioned in course 2, treaties during this period can be called comprehensive land claims or modern treaties. These treaties deal with the unfinished business of treaty making in Canada.
