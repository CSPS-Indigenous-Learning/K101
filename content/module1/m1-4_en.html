Question of

True or False?

First Nations are present in Canada in every province and territory.

Answer: False

First Nations are present in Canada from coast to coast and everywhere in between with the exception of the Territory of Nunavut which is populated primarily by Inuit.

Question of

True or False?

Three-quarters of the nation’s Indigenous languages are endangered.

Answer: True

Today, only Cree, Inuktitut and Ojibwa are thought to have enough speakers to be sustained indefinitely.

Question of

True or False?

When possible, when talking to First Nations people, be specific and name the Community or Nation. And if you don’t know, it is always better to ask than to assume.

Answer: True

When asking questions about names and experiences, we have to remember that not everyone feels comfortable explaining the personal significance of names and experiences. Timing and context are important when learning about one another, which is a large part of building respectful relationships.
