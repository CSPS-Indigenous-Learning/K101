Much of Quebec and British Columbia are unceded and unsurrendered territory.

The Courts in Canada have continually recognized that Indigenous groups retain rights and titles on the land where they have resided from time immemorial. Ongoing negotiations have gone on for decades with no end in sight. This is very costly for the Government of Canada and Canadians.
